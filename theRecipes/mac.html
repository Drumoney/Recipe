Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Water
  • Pot
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Fill a pot of water
  2. Place the pot on the stove
  3. Place the macaroni in the pot
  4. Let boil
  5. Take water out pot
  6. Put the cheese on the Macaroni
